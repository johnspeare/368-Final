cycling

I love riding bikes. I like them because they're fun -- all of the other benefits (and there are many) are secondary to the fun part. I commute mostly. But when I have time I like to take longer rides -- a day, or maybe a few days. My favorite riding it to explore the quiet and beautiful dirt roads of the National and State forests in Washington with friends.
